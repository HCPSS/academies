Certifications

Upon completion of the Computer Networking pathway experience, students will be prepared to take the Cisco Certified Network Engineer Technician (CCENT), Cisco Certified Network Administrator (CCNA), and the CompTIA Security + certification exams.

Upon completion of the PC Systems pathway experience, students will be prepared to take the CompTIA A+, Cisco Certified Network Engineer Technician (CCENT) and CompTIA Net + certification exam.
